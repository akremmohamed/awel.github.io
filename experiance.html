Recommended Resources

Books, documents and materials that helped me grow as a developer

Programming Fundamentals

Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship

by Robert C. Martin

Essential reading for writing maintainable, efficient code. Covers principles that apply to all programming languages.

View on Amazon

The Pragmatic Programmer

by Andrew Hunt and David Thomas

Timeless advice for becoming a more effective programmer, with practical tips and best practices.

View on Amazon

Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software

by Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, John Vlissides

The classic "Gang of Four" book that introduced design patterns to software development.

View on Amazon

by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein

Comprehensive coverage of algorithms with mathematical rigor. Known as "CLRS".

by Harold Abelson, Gerald Jay Sussman

Classic MIT textbook that teaches fundamental principles of computing.
